OUR SERVICES

WATER TREATMENT DESIGN, BUILD/ASSEMBLE, SALES & INSTALLATIONS

ANALCHEM-YGT HYDRO TECHNOLOGIES LTD offers a highly technical unbiased approach in the competitive Design/Selection, Build/Assemble as well as monitor implementation/installation of the most suitable treatment plant technology that will adapt to the characteristics (hydraulics, physico-chemical & bacteriological) of the raw water, the final desired treated water use and demand. We employ the “Multi-Barrier Principle” as recommended by the World Health Organization (WHO) in our approach to water treatment in order to adequately provide a sustainable, affordable, adequate coverage, quantitative and qualitative (The United Nations-UN 5 Water Supply Elements) water supply, avoiding the use of “All-in-One” treatment systems.

1. Pre/Post-treatment (oxidation/disinfection)

Whether oxidizable chemical or bacteriological contaminants are present in the source water or not (to avoid ingress/leachate or cross contamination), we recommend for oxidation/disinfection with oxidizing/disinfecting agent in form of either off-site/on-site chlorine based (chlorination) or on-site generated (Corona Discharge/Ultra-Violet UV) Ozone (ozonation) or combined injection into the raw water from source through a venturi/mixer and/or filter module/diffuser.

Adsorption filtration utilizing activated carbon/anthracite as media is employed to remove color, taste and odor imparting organic contaminants including chlorine odor in water and DBPs.

2. Granular (Particle) and Adsorption Pressure Filtering Plants:

Granular or Particle filtration is employed for the separation of suspended/colloidal solids/particles from water either originally present in the water body or precipitated as a result of oxidation process of oxidizable contaminants. The particle filtration media utilized depending on the water chemistry may be an oxidizing media, multi-media, or sand media.

Adsorption filtration utilizing activated carbon/anthracite as media is employed to remove color, taste and odor imparting organic contaminants including chlorine odor in water and DBPs. In each case, gravel (coarse/pebble) as a media supporting bed is included in either pressure vessels (rapid filters) or gravity filtration units.

3. Membrane Particle Filtration Systems

Membrane particle filtration systems composed of Micro-MF and/or Ultra-UF filtration. These are also employed for the removal of suspended/colloidal particles from water and in most cases, without the need for addition of coagulants/flocculation agents. These membrane filters may be as “Immersed (Hollow Fiber) Cassette or Membrane Cartridge Modules”.

We offer Cartridge Micron (5.0, 1.0, 0.5, 0.1 & Carbon Block) Filters with either Plastic or Stainless Steel (SS) Housings. Standard or Jumbo sizes of cartridges are available in Single or Multi Style Housings.

4. Nano-Filtration and Pressure Softeners

We Build/Assemble Nano-Filtration Systems to replace the conventional Pressure Softeners for the removal of Water Hardness (Membrane Softening) as well as removal of Total Organic Carbon-TOC/Heavy Molecular Weight Organic Acids (Tannins-Humic & Fulvic Acids) that impart yellowish-Brown coloration to water, especially from underground/borehole sources.

These are available for minimum flowrate of 850 Gallons Per Day (GPD)/134 Litres Per Hour (LPH). We also assemble the conventional Pressure Softeners using FRP pressure vessels either as Stand-Alone or integrated with Particle Filtering Units. They are either fitted with Manual Multi-Port Control Valves (Top or Side mounted) OR with Electro-Mechanical Control Valves.

5. Reverse Osmosis-RO Systems

We Build RO systems to suite your water chemistry and demand. Our Brackish Water RO systems range from domestic up to Industrial/Commercial needs. The RO Housings may be SS or FRP on either Mild Steel coated with Red Oxide anti-corrosion and Marine Grade Paints Frames or on SS Frames.

  • OZONE GENERATORS

  • PARTICLE FILTRATION SYSTEMS

  • ULTRAFILTRATION (UF) SYSTEMS

  • REVERSE OSMOSIS (RO) SYSTEMS

ENVIRONMENTAL

1. Quality Surveillance/Pollution Monitoring & Analytical Services

Under this programme, we undertake Quality and Pollution Assessment of Water sources and treated water; air and soil from plant systems as a component of sanitation programme and source protection with a view to avoiding public health problems/enhancing good healthcare; compliance with environmental discharge regulations; efficient plant operation/elimination of production constraints and extended equipment life.

2. Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)

We undertake environmental and ecological impacts assessment of projects from conception to commissioning in order to ensure that projects are implemented with maximum consideration for the environment using the following tools:

  • Project Proposal
  • Scoping (Term of Reference (TOR) for Scope of EIA Study/Preliminary Assessment Report; Public Hearing; EIA Study)
  • Draft EIA Report
  • Financial EIA Report
  • Mitigation Compliance Monitoring
  • Environmental Auditing/Post Impact Assessment Studies (Post EIA)

We undertake EIA for the following mandatory list: Petroleum (Upstream & Downstream; Waste Treatment & Disposal; Water Supply/Treatment; Power Generation/Transmission; Food & Beverages etc.

3. Compliance Monitoring, Inspection & Supervision Services

Evaluation/feasibility study of the current conditions of systems is one of our major activities. we undertake physical inspection of process equipment, operational records and carry out wide spectrum analytical services with a view to evaluating the current condition, compliance to regulatory bodies in product and waste discharge qualities, areas of reliability improvement, performance optimization, reducing maintenance/operation costs, boosting profit and extent of rehabilitation /revamping or modification works of your existing purification/ treatment facilities. We identify clear financial benefits that any performance and reliability improvement will guarantee and then highlight key performance indicators (KPIs)/Critical Control Points (CCP) that will be used to measure benefits/safety performance.

4. Autopsies/Foulant Analysis & Resins/Membrane Cleaning

Surface of RO membranes, ion exchange resins and internals of heat exchangers are subject to fouling by foreign materials such as hydrates of metal oxides, calcium precipitates, organics and biological matter. Fouling is a term that includes the build-up of all kinds of layers including scaling, slime formation/adhesion (bio-fouling) and corrosion. Our Autopsy and Foulant Analysis provides accurate foulant identification, eliminating the guesswork in pinpointing the source of recurring water treatment problems such as corrosion/ scaling, bio-fouling etc. We have the capacity to undertake RO membrane through the use of Clean-In-Place (CIP) and resin cleaning.
